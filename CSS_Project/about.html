The Story of Eduford

Eduford was established as a niche computer training institute in 1986. It was founded by Mr.Hein Min Htet.Eduford was reorganized, restructured, and rose in the market as a Private Limited Company focused on delivering ICT Solutions.

Today, Eduford is the largest one-stop IT Products and Services Provider in Myanmar, with a well-established workforce of over 650+.Eduford has a large customer base, including ongoing relationships with major Government Departments and Private Enterprises, and has graduated over 85000 students through Eduford Education.
